Cryptocurrencies, unlike traditional money (also called fiat) are digital assets that are not controlled by any third party, like a bank, government or institution. This means that you, and only you, actually own the money. It also means that everybody is welcomed in the system as there’s no central authority controlling who will be accepted and not.

Instead transactions are verified and secured by cryptography, or simply put, code. The Internet once transformed the way we exchange information and crypto is right now doing the same for the movement of value. We’re not kidding when we say it’s revolutionary.

Safello is Sweden's leading online cryptocurrency brokerage and has been paving the way for crypto adoption since 2013.

Our goal is to make cryptocurrencies accessible for everyone and we offer a secure way to buy, sell and store crypto in seamless transactions at industry-leading speeds.

Operating in Sweden, Safello has been registered as a financial institution with Finansinspektionen (Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority) since 2013 and has been listed at Nasdaq First North Growth Market since 2021.
